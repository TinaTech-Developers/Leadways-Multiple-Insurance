Short Term Businesses

Insurance is a contract, represented by a policy, in which a policyholder receives financial protection or reimbursement against losses from an insurer in exchange of a premium. It is a way to manage your risk, when you buy insurance, you purchase protection against unexpected financial losses. In most cases, the policy holder pays part of the loss (called the excess), and the insurer pays the rest. At Leadways Multiple Agent we offer a wide range of products, which are tailor made to meet the exclusive and distinct needs of the insuring community. Below are some of the products and services for individuals and organizations.

1. Fire Policies

These policies offer protection against loss or damage to property/assets caused by fire and allied perils. The sum insured under these policies should be equal to the replacement value of that property/asset. It is advised that the sum insured be reviewed from time to time to allow you to enjoy full cover. Perils covered are as follows: - Fire, lightning, explosion/implosion, riot, strike, malicious damage, impact by aircraft, flood, storm, tempest, subsidence, earthquake, landslide. The fire policy comes in different packages depending on the particular client's needs. Below is a brief description of the policies available:

a. Assets All Risks

This is suitable for medium to large corporates with a sizeable asset base. The policy requires that the sums insured be reflective of the replacement value and the premium is usually calculated based on the material damage limit. The other sections covered include theft as defined in the policy, money, transit, glass, personal effects of employees; all risks section which covers specified items like cell phones, bicycles. The cover further extends to consequential loss (loss of profit) and accounts receivable. These sections basically compensate the insured for loss of income which could have been realized if the loss had not occurred. Other losses covered under this section include claims preparation costs, fines and penalties, costs of hiring alternative premises, wages and salaries and other fixed expenses. The Policy Extends to Cover:
• Non-Political Riot strike and civil commotion
• Property in the open
• Temporary removal of property
• Capital additions up to 15% of sum insured
• Exclusions
• Loss of earnings, loss by delay, loss of market or other consequential or indirect loss or damage of any kind of description, whatsoever.
• Loss or damage resulting from total or partial cessation of work or the retardation or interruption or cessation of any process or operations.

b. Business Protection Policy

This policy targets medium corporate clients, who might be involved in manufacturing, processing or stocking products for sale. Besides covering the buildings and contents, extensions can be inserted to cover money, burglary, glass, and public liability. This policy can also be taken up by an organization using rented premises. Many sections of the Assets All Risks can be brought into this policy; the main difference is on the asset base.

c. Office Comprehensive

Mainly intended for medium to large corporate clients with a sizeable asset base. It covers office furniture and equipment. However, extensions for money, burglary, glass, and public liability can be inserted upon request.

2. Motor Insurance

It is a requirement at law that every vehicle must have insurance before using public roads. At Leadways Multiple Agent we offer three options through which you can insure your vehicle:

a) Full Third Party:

Basically, covers your legal liability to third parties for causing damage to their property or death and bodily injury.

b) Full Third Party, Fire and Theft:

Further to the cover offered by full third party, it extends to cover any damage or loss caused by fire, theft or attempted theft.

c) Comprehensive Cover:

This covers loss of or damage to a vehicle due to accidents, theft, fire, self-ignition, riots and liability to third parties for damage to property or death/bodily injury.

d) Motor Fleet Cover:

This provides comprehensive cover at highly competitive rates for companies or individuals with more than twenty vehicles.

e) Motor Traders (internal & external)

This covers motor traders against liability to third parties. Also covered are the vehicles on sale against all risks

3. Group Personal Accident

The policy is usually provided by the employer. It provides worldwide 24-hour cover for injury or death of employees following an accident caused by violent, visible and external means. The benefits attached to the policy are:
• Death
• Temporary Total Disablement
• Permanent Disablement
• Medical expenses.

4. Liabilities (legal, product, tenants, public, general and employers)

Provides insurance coverage to protect against claims alleging that one's negligence or inappropriate action resulting in bodily injury or property damage.

5. Umbrella Liability

A type of liability insurance available to individuals and companies protecting them against claims over and above the amount covered by their primary policies.

6. Fidelity Guarantee

Covers the insured against financial loss (money or property) as a direct result of an act of fraud or dishonesty committed by its employees. Employees covered are those individuals whom the insured has the right at all times to govern, control, and direct in the performance of their work. Contract workers are also included.

7. Goods in Transit

The policy covers the insured against loss of or damage to goods due to fire, collision and or overturning in the course of transit by means of road or rail or being stored during a journey within the territorial limits of Zimbabwe.

8. Marine Cargo

It compensates for financial losses resulting from loss of or damage to goods arising out of maritime disaster. The policy may also be extended to cover losses arising from road conveyance from the warehouse of the exporter to the port and / or from the port to the warehouse of the importer. The policy offers worldwide coverage.

9. Marine Hull

This covers loss of or damage to the hull and machinery of shipping or fishing vessels, crafts on the lakes while on the high seas or the water masses within Zimbabwe.

10. Bonds and Guarantees

A bond or guarantee can be described as a written understanding by the guarantor to accept responsibility for the performance of a contractual obligation entered into by the contractor liable under the contract in the event of the contractor defaulting.
• Purposes of Bonds and Guarantees
• Providing a secure foundation on which to build business:

10.a) Bid Bond

This bond ascertains the seriousness of the contractor. It is seen as an assurance by the contractors that the tender procedures will be followed.

10.b) Performance Bond

This is a guarantee of a contractor's performance of his contractual obligations under a contract. Non-performance by the contractor can come in various forms such as:
• Failure to execute the entire job
• Defective performance
• Breach of contract's terms or conditions
• Failure to complete in time.

10.c) Advance payment Bond

This bond is required to guard against the misuse of money that is paid in advance to contractors for the purchase of materials.

10.d) Retention Bond

This bond enables the retained monies to be released to the contractor, at the same time guaranteeing the employer that in the event of default by the contractor, such monies will be paid to the employer.

10.e) Maintenance Bond

This bond covers the cost of defects that are fixed when the contractor fails to return to site after the completion of the contract

11. Professional Indemnity

This policy provides cover for claims brought against the policyholder due to their professional negligence.

12. Contactors All Risks

The policy offers comprehensive and adequate protection against loss or damage in respect of contract works, construction plant and equipment and/ or construction machinery, as well as third party claims in respect of property damage or bodily injury arising in connection with the execution of a civil engineering project. This policy is extremely useful for consulting engineers, architects and financiers because it contributes to reducing the overall construction expenses and at the same time offers efficient financial protection for all parties concerned. Covered Risks: Contractor's All Risk provides 'all risk' cover unless the hazard is specifically excluded. It covers the risk of fire, lightning, explosion, flood, inundation, rain, snow, earthquake, theft, burglary, bad workmanship, lack of skill, negligence, human error.
Major Exclusions:
This policy is subject to the following exclusions: - loss or damage due to nuclear reaction, consequential loss, loss or damage due to faulty design, loss or damage due to war or war-like operation etc

13. Plant All Risks

It covers against unforeseen and sudden physical loss or damage to insured's plant from all risks including fire, theft and accidents or any other causes included in the policy.

14. Passenger Liability

It covers against legal liability for death and bodily injury to persons while being carried in or upon entering or alighting from an insured vehicle.

15. Directors Personal Accident

This policy covers death or bodily injury to directors caused by accidental, violent and visible external means.

Engineering

a) Electronic Equipment This policy is ideal for individuals or corporate organizations that use electronic equipment for purposes of business or pleasure. This insurance policy covers electronic and ancillary equipment against the following risks:
• Location perils and natural calamities
• Theft, burglary or housekeeping
• Damage whilst in transit or
• Riot, strike or malicious damage
Electronic equipment as defined in the policy includes computers, microprocessors, word processors, telecommunication equipment, electronic machinery meant for medical use and other electronic equipment used in film and television studios, electronic scoreboards etc. This insurance provides compensation based on certain specific circumstances and these form the sections of the policy: Material damage: This section basically covers the physical structure of the electronic equipment against loss or damage as a result of the above perils
External data media: The sum insured should be the amount required for restoring the insured's external data media by replacing with new materials and reproducing lost information as a result of a material damage loss. Increased cost of working: After a loss the insured might have to hire substitute equipment or hire additional labour to reproduce the lost information. The costs are covered under this section.
a) Machinery Breakdown/Plant All Risks Policies
This insurance is ideal for all owners of plant and machinery since it protects them from all major risks and contingencies that may arise and cause unforeseen losses and physical damages to their property. Cover is provided whether the plant and machinery are at work, at rest, in the process of being dismantled for cleaning or overhauling, being shifted within the premises or in the process of being re-erected. Compensation is offered in the form of replacement subject to the sum insured being equal to the replacement value. If plant and machinery need to be shifted from one location to another, then transit cover must be obtained, however the policy can be extended to cover transit, third party liability, clearance and removal of debris.

17. Farming Comprehensive

This covers irrigation equipment, farming equipment, infrastructure, livestock, crops etc against loss or damage as a result of fire, lightning, explosion, storm, flooding, and impact by animals and aircraft, pipe bursts and break ins. This also covers livestock against losses sustained as a result of fire, lightning and electrocution.

18. Credit Insurance Cover

Is for default of payment due to unforeseen circumstances e.g. death, permanent disablement or illness.

• Fire, lightning, thunderbolts, explosion
• Non-political riot strikes and malicious damage
• Storm, tempest and flooding

Householders (Contents) To protect movable personal property, mostly household contents. This encompasses all items from curtains to books in the shelves. TRAVEL INSURANCE

Travel insurance provides you with the best coverage for medical expenses, trip cancellation/interruption, baggage damage, medical evacuation, and much more. Plus, you will also receive 24-hour travel assistance.

COMESA YELLOW CARDS

The Yellow Card is essentially a Regional third-party motor vehicle insurance Scheme that provides third party legal liability cover and compensation for medical expenses resulting from road traffic accidents caused by visiting motorists. Besides offering third party liability protection to the insured or the driver whilst in a foreign country, the COMESA Yellow Card Scheme also offers emergency medical cover to the driver and passengers of the foreign motor vehicle involved in the traffic accident. The third-party liability cover offered is limited to the statute provisions on road traffic third party liability award limits of that country being visited and in which the incident has happened from.
